Recently I have been engaged with two main projects, both of which use a similar methodology of numerical simulation of population dynamics models:

  • Characteristics of spatial eco-evolutionary meta-communities and their response to perturbation.
  • Simulating COVID-19 in Northern Ireland using age-structured SEIIR epidemiological models.

Future work plans:

  • Spatial ecological modelling of food webs and ecosystems in the Upper Don valley of the Peak District.
  • Evolving ideological dynamics in social networks.
